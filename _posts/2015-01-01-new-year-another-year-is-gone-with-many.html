--- title: New Year date: '2015-01-01T20:42:00.000-08:00' author: kahnchana tags: - New Year - poetry modified_time: '2016-08-01T22:02:26.426-07:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-1106450633556051873.post-9145937480116079835 blogger_orig_url: https://kahnchana.blogspot.com/2015/01/new-year-another-year-is-gone-with-many.html --- Another year is gone with many a merry,
Days and nights embedded deep in memory,
Colourful days with many an amazing friend,
All good things must come to an end,
Yet what's gone's not to fret and cry,
But to learn your faults to stand up high.

Another year is gone but another comes ahead,
Filled with mystery and days unknown instead,
May be joy may be pains the coming year,
Be made of, but it's not for us to fear,
All one needs is friends in life to love and cheer,
Let me wish you and all have a  happy new year!
