After a long nap on the bus from Heathrow to Cambridge, arrived here at about 1.30. The evening was spent quite enjoyably. Joined a couple of Tamara's friends to watch the UK Panama match: a massacre at 6-1. Afterwards, visited Jesus College, and after one interesting chat at the cafeteria, did a detailed tour around the Campus. We came across a book sale at the library where I bought two great books really cheap. Later on, took a tour around the city, visiting Sidney Sussex College, Corpus Christi College and a couple other places around the city. Ended the evening watching swans on the riverside Anchor pub. Interesting first day at Cambridge.
